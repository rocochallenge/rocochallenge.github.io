RoCo Challenge@AAAI 2026 Schedule

Onsite timeline for teams, judges, and organizers

Day 1

Team Setup & Calibration

Jan 24, 2026

Time Activity Duration Description
8:40 - 9:00 Welcome & Briefing 20 min Opening remarks, competition overview, and safety protocol introduction.
9:00 - 9:30 System Registration & Workspace Assignment 30 min Team check-in, workspace allocation, and hardware verification.
9: 30 - 14:00 Team Calibration Sessions (Teams 1–3) 3 × 1.5 h Each team receives a 2-hour slot for setup, robot calibration, and HRI protocol rehearsal.
14:00 - 15:30 Lunch / Networking Break ~1.5 h Informal interaction among teams, judges, and organizers.
15:30 - 20:00 Team Calibration Sessions (Teams 4–6) 3 × 1.5 h Environment integration, perception tuning, and dry-run validation for remaining teams.
20:00 - 21:00 System Integration Check 6 h Unified review to confirm system readiness and onsite integration status.
21:00 – 24:00 Late Arrival Deployment & Integration Testing 3 h Reserved testing and deployment window for teams unable to arrive or complete system setup during the scheduled daytime sessions.
Note: Day 1 runs on a slot-based schedule. Exact start times and team slot assignments will be announced soon.
Day 2

Onsite Finals & Demonstrations

Jan 25, 2026

Time Activity Duration Description
09:00 – 09:30 Final Briefing & Readiness Review 30 min Confirmation of competition environment, evaluation procedure, and final equipment checks.
09:30 – 12:30 Model Testing Phase 3 h Execution of all submitted final models to verify result reproducibility and ensure evaluation fairness.
12:30 – 13:30 Lunch Break 1 h Lunch and rest period.
13:30 – 17:30 Official Onsite Trials 4 h Formal onsite demonstrations conducted in sequence; task success rate and assembly accuracy are recorded.
17:30 – 18:30 Metric Computation & Feedback 1 h Final score computation, log review, and ranking preparation by the evaluation committee.
After 18:30 Day 2 Wrap-up – End of Day 2 schedule.
Note: All times are local onsite time unless otherwise announced by organizers.
Day 3

Awards & Technical Talks (AAAI Venue)

Jan 26, 2026

Time Activity Duration Description
14:45 – 14:55 Award Ceremony @ AAAI Venue ~10 min Top 3 teams recognized at the AAAI HCM Workshop plenary; certificates and prizes presented.
